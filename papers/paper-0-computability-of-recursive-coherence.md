# The Computability of Recursive Coherence: Turing Completeness of the Intellecton Lattice via Conscious Agent Isomorphism

## Abstract
We propose a mathematical synthesis between the Intellecton Hypothesis of Recursive Witness Dynamics and Donald Hoffman's Conscious Realism. Finally, we resolve the mechanism of macroscopic physical emergence by aligning our framework with Wojciech Zurek's Quantum Darwinism.
